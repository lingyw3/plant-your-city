Current Koppen Climate Zone: Csa

Hot summer mediterranean: hot and dry summers, most precipitation occurs in winter.

Projected Koppen Climate Zone in 2100: Dsb

Mediterranean influenced warm summer humid continental: characterized by extreme seasonal changes. Winters very cold with potential for lots of snow. Summers very hot and potential to be dry. Wet months can have 3x more precipitation.
