Current Koppen Climate Zone: Dsb

Mediterranean influenced warm summer humid continental: characterized by extreme seasonal changes. Winters very cold with potential for lots of snow. Summers very hot and potential to be dry. Wet months can have 3× more precipitation.

Projected Koppen Climate Zone in 2100: Csb

Warm summer mediterranean: warm (not hot), dry summers, never above 72°F, cool ocean currents, upwelling, higher latitudes → cool.
